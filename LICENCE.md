### BAFU — Federal Office for the Environment

Hydrological safety data (flow danger levels 1–5, threshold definitions) is
sourced from the Swiss Federal Office for the Environment
([BAFU / OFEV](https://www.hydrodaten.admin.ch)). This data is official Swiss
public safety information and must be presented accurately and without
modification of safety thresholds. See `CONSTITUTION.md` Article VIII.

Weather forecast data is sourced from MeteoSwiss

and forecast data are subject to MeteoSwiss terms of use.
